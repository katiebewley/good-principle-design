TEN
PRINCIPLES
OF
GOOD DESIGN
BY
DIETER RAMS

  1. 1

    Good design is innovative
    Design is to constantly
    strive for authenticity and
    should be infinitely be redone.
  2. 2

    Good design makes a product useful
    Product functionality should reflect
    not only physical function but also
    should show a strong cognitive use.
  3. 3

    Good design is aesthetic
    Although the product should
    be functional, the design
    should be as minimal as possible.
  4. 4

    Good design makes a product understandable
    The design should be simple to
    apphrehend and showcase immediate
    perception of the use.
  5. 5

    Good design is honest
    The product does not
    mislead or prove
    an opposing point.
  6. 6

    Good design is unobtrusive
    The composition should not
    be overdone, as too many
    formats can retract from
    the meaning.
  7. 7

    Good design is long-lasting
    Innovative design depicts
    the longevity of and
    relativeness in
    the past and future.
  8. 8

    Good design is thorough down to the last detail
    Down to the aesthetics,
    every detail must be
    cohesive and strong.
  9. 9

    Good design is environmentally friendly
    The product should benefit
    and be sustainable
    to our society and world.
  10. 10

    Good design is as little design as possible
    The more minimal,
    the more the user will
    able to understand
    the design.
